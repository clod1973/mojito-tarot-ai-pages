Privacy Policy

Mojito Tarot AI
Last updated: 24 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Mojito Tarot AI collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app. Mojito Tarot AI provides tarot readings for entertainment, self-reflection, and personal insight only.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the app, we may collect the following information:

  • Name, if provided through Apple or Google sign-in.
  • Email address used for authentication and account functionality.
  • User ID created by Firebase Authentication.
  • Tarot questions, selected cards, and AI-generated reading results.
  • Purchase-related information such as product ID, question credits, and transaction-derived state for app functionality.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide sign-in and account functionality.
  • Save and display tarot readings.
  • Generate AI tarot readings from your questions and selected cards.
  • Manage purchased question credits.
  • Support account deletion and app support requests.
  • Maintain app security and reliability.

Authentication

Mojito Tarot AI supports sign-in with Apple, Google, and email/password. Authentication is handled through Firebase Authentication. Your sign-in provider may share basic account information, such as name and email address, depending on your settings.

Tarot Questions and AI Readings

When you request a tarot reading, your question and selected card details are sent to a Firebase Cloud Function. The backend may use OpenAI services to generate the reading response. Readings may be stored in Firebase Firestore so you can access app functionality connected to your account.

In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. Mojito Tarot AI does not receive or store your credit card number or Apple payment credentials. The app stores purchase-related state needed to grant and manage question credits.

Third-Party Services

Mojito Tarot AI uses the following services:

  • Apple services for Sign in with Apple and in-app purchases.
  • Google Sign-In for optional authentication.
  • Firebase Authentication for account sign-in, including email/password access.
  • Firebase Firestore for app data storage.
  • Firebase Cloud Functions for backend processing.
  • OpenAI for AI-generated tarot reading responses through the backend.

Tracking and Advertising

Mojito Tarot AI does not use your data for tracking and does not use the Apple advertising identifier. The app does not show third-party advertising.

Account Deletion

You can request account deletion from the Profile tab in the app. Deleting your account removes your sign-in account from Mojito Tarot AI where technically possible. Some purchase records may remain with Apple as required for App Store purchase history and legal compliance.

Data Security

We use standard security measures provided by Apple, Google, and Firebase to help protect account and app data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect information used by the app.

Children

Mojito Tarot AI is intended for users aged 18 or older. The app is not intended for children.

Entertainment Disclaimer

Mojito Tarot AI provides tarot interpretations for entertainment, self-reflection, and personal insight only. It does not provide medical, legal, financial, psychological, mental health, or professional advice.
